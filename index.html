Paula's Classic Sinigang Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 pound pork ribs, cut into serving pieces
  • 1 large eggplant, sliced
  • 1 bundle string beans (sitaw), cut into 2-inch pieces
  • 2 medium-sized radishes, sliced
  • 2 medium-sized tomatoes, quartered
  • 1 large red onion, sliced
  • 2 pieces green chili peppers (siling haba)
  • 1 pack sinigang mix (tamarind soup base)
  • 8 cups water
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. 1. In a large pot, bring 8 cups of water to a boil.
  2. 2. Add the pork ribs and simmer until tender.
  3. 3. Add the sliced eggplant, string beans, radishes, tomatoes, and onions.
  4. 4. Stir in the sinigang mix and let it simmer until the vegetables are tender.
  5. 5. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  6. 6. Add the green chili peppers for a bit of heat (adjust according to your preference).
  7. 7. Simmer for a few more minutes until all ingredients are cooked through.
  8. 8. Adjust the seasoning if necessary, and serve hot.
  9. 9. Enjoy your delicious sinigang!
